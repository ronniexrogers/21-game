Game Rules

  • Each player starts out with two cards.
  • In this game, it is player vs dealer.
  • The objective is to get your total cards value as close to 21 without going over.
  • If you'd like an additional card, press "Hit me!"
  • If your total cards value is greater than 21, you automatically lose.
  • If you don't want anymore cards, press "Stand."
  • After you stand, the dealer will reveal their cards.
  • If the dealer's initial total is less than 17, they keep drawing until their total is greater than 17.
  • If the dealer's total cards value is greater than 21, you win!
  • If the dealer's total is the same as your total, it's a draw!
